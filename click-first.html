·The system, lever by lever

On their own, each lever plateaus. Together, they amplify each other.

A beautiful site without SEO stays invisible. A Google profile without a website doesn't convert. Content without structured data isn't read by AI. Here are the components of Click First™, and how each one feeds the others.

Website & Technical SEO

The foundation of your presence, and the technical base Google evaluates first. A fast, mobile-first, clean site is one that engines index and rank easily, and that your customers keep open.

  • Custom, conversion-focused design
  • Core Web Vitals in the green, speed and CDN
  • Architecture, internal linking, canonicals, sitemap
  • Stack adapted to your needs (WordPress, Brizy Cloud, static, custom)

Local organic SEO

We build your authority to rank sustainably on business-intent searches, the ones that bring a customer, not just a visitor. Service pages, location pages, geo-targeted keywords.

  • Technical and on-page audit
  • Service + city keywords
  • Content that answers real searches

Google Business Profile

Often the very first contact with your future customers, and the most powerful local lever. We optimize your Google profile and sync it with your site to dominate Google Maps and the local pack.

  • Complete profile, categories and services
  • Geotagged photos and regular posts
  • Profile and website in sync

Local citations & NAP consistency

NAP stands for Name, Address, Phone. Google trusts a business more when this information is rigorously identical everywhere. We align your citations across local directories and platforms.

  • Identical NAP across site, profile and directories
  • Relevant local citations
  • Cleanup of existing inconsistencies

Google Reviews

The social proof your customers check before choosing you, and a strong prominence signal for Google and AI alike. We put in place a strategy to earn and manage reviews.

  • Review-generation strategy
  • Replies and online reputation
  • Highlighted on the website

Structured data: Schema.org & OKF

Schema.org markup describes your business in a language Google understands. OKF, an AI-readable knowledge format, goes further: it structures your facts so ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity cite you accurately.

  • Schema LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Breadcrumb
  • OKF bundle, llms.txt, Entity Homes
  • Consistent named entities

AI visibility (GEO)

GEO, Generative Engine Optimization, prepares your presence for generative engines. Tomorrow, your customers will ask an AI for advice. We configure your content and your crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) so they find you and recommend you.

  • Clear, citable content
  • AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude
  • AI crawler configuration

StoryBrand content

A clear message where your customer is the hero and you are the guide. The StoryBrand method clarifies what you do in five seconds, reassures, converts, and feeds both SEO and AI understanding.

  • Clarified message (StoryBrand)
  • Service pages and location pages
  • Conversion-focused content

Continuous optimization

Your visibility is a living organism. Google evolves, AI evolves, search evolves. We monitor, adjust and improve every month, with a clear KPI report on what's progressing.

  • Monthly content and SEO updates
  • Tracking of rankings and AI mentions
  • Transparent KPI report

What about CRM automation?

Online booking, lead tracking and automated follow-ups aren't part of the Click First™ core. It's an add-on service we offer only to businesses that genuinely need it, so no contact is lost once visibility is in place.
