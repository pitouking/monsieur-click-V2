Redesign vs new website: what is the difference?

If you have no site yet, our web design service starts from scratch with the right foundations. A redesign starts from what you already have: we keep what works (ranking pages, performing content, domain equity) and rebuild what blocks you (speed, architecture, unclear message). Surgical work — not a blind wipe.

SEO migration without ranking loss

The number-one fear in a redesign is losing Google rankings. It is fair — and avoidable. We plan every 301, keep strategic URLs when possible, migrate performing content and submit the new sitemap in Search Console. A 30–60 day post-launch check confirms indexing and rankings stabilise.
