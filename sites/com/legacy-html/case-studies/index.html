Local SEO & website case studies

Seven businesses that went from invisible to obvious on Google and AI with Click First™.

Each case study documents the problem, the solution and measurable outcomes. One system: website, local SEO, Google Business Profile, reviews, Schema.org and GEO.

These seven builds span fitness, medical massage, law, close protection, coaching, a recording studio and a craft business. What they share: a competent company that was hard to find online, then a Click First™ ecosystem aligned for Google, Maps and AI.

Every card shows context (city, FR or US market), Click First™ score when available, concrete results and the levers we activated. We do not promise position 1: we show real trajectories with a transparent method.

Typical levers include StoryBrand messaging, local landing pages, Google Business Profile optimization, review strategy, Schema.org markup and GEO for AI assistants. Open any case study to see the full before/after narrative.

Whether you run a fitness club in France, a medical practice in the US or a craft business in Portland, the mechanics are the same: clarify the offer, align website and Google listing, then measure progress every month.
