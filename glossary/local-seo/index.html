Definition

Local SEO

Local SEO is the set of actions that help a business appear in location-based search: Google Maps, the local pack (the three map results at the top), and queries like "service + city".

It mainly rests on proximity, relevance and prominence. In practice: a complete Google Business Profile, steady reviews, consistent NAP (name, address, phone), an aligned website and local content.
