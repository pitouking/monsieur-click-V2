SEO & GEO Glossary

Clear, citable definitions for Google and AI engines. Three entries to start.

  • Local SEO

    Local SEO is the set of actions that help a business appear in location-based search: Google Maps, the local pack (the three map results at the top), and queries like "service + city".

  • Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

    Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) structures your online presence so AI systems cite you as a trustworthy source when people ask for recommendations.

  • Google Business Profile

    Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is a business's official listing on Google: Maps, the local pack, the knowledge panel and often the first mobile touchpoint.
