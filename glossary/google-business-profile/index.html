Google Business Profile

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is a business's official listing on Google: Maps, the local pack, the knowledge panel and often the first mobile touchpoint.

A complete profile (categories, hours, photos, description, services, posts) plus fresh reviews builds trust with Google and with customers. It is a pillar of local SEO.
