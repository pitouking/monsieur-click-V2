Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) structures your online presence so AI systems cite you as a trustworthy source when people ask for recommendations.

ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews lean on clear content, consistent entities, Schema.org structured data, reviews and verifiable facts. GEO is not the opposite of SEO: it builds on the same foundation, made more citable.
