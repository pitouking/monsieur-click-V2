How do you show up in the answers of ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity?

81|81|81|81|

TL;DR – There's no button to sign your business up in ChatGPT or Gemini. AI recommends businesses that publish useful content, inspire trust, have a consistent digital identity and are recognized as references in their field.

82|82|82|82|
83|83|83|83|
84|84|84|84| 85|85|85|85|
86|86|86|86|
87|87|87|87| 88|88|88|88|
89|89|89|89| 90|90|90|90|

A few years ago, the question we heard most was: how do I appear on Google's first page? Today, a new question is gaining importance: how do I get ChatGPT or another AI to recommend my business?

91|91|91|91|

This shift is logical. Habits are changing. Instead of scanning a list of links, many people ask directly which is the best SEO consultant, which tradesperson is recommended near them or which firm to choose. The answers are now often provided by an AI. The good news is that you can act to increase your chances of appearing there.

92|92|92|92|

Can you sign your business up in ChatGPT?

93|93|93|93|

No. There's no official form to add your business to ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude or Perplexity. AI assistants don't work like a directory. They rely on the information available across the web. They analyze content, reliable sources, a business's reputation and the consistency of the information they find.

94|94|94|94|

In other words, you can't ask to be recommended. You have to become recommendable.

95|95|95|95|

How does AI choose its answers?

96|96|96|96|

Even though each engine has its own workings, they all look for the same qualities. They favor businesses that demonstrate clear expertise, useful content, a consistent identity, a good reputation, reliable information and an identifiable specialization. They mainly seek to answer the user's question correctly, exactly like how Google ranks businesses.

97|97|97|97|

Visibility in AI starts with a good site

98|98|98|98|

Your site remains the heart of your digital presence, but it must evolve. It can no longer just present your business. It must answer the questions your future customers ask. Each page must give a clear answer, each article must solve a specific problem and each piece of content must demonstrate your expertise. The more useful your site becomes, the more credibility it gains.

99|99|99|99|

Educational content has become essential

100|100|100|100|

AI especially appreciates content that explains: why a problem exists, what the solutions are, which criteria to compare, which mistakes to avoid and how to make a good decision. This content perfectly meets users' expectations. It's also easy to summarize, which increases its citation potential.

101|101|101|101|

Entities help AI understand your business

102|102|102|102|

Modern engines no longer just read keywords. They seek to identify entities: your business, your services, your city, your specialties, your products and your partners. The more consistent this information, the easier your business is to understand.

103|103|103|103|

Your brand becomes an entity

The more consistently your brand appears on your site, on Google Business Profile, in directories, on professional networks and in specialized content, the more clearly AI identifies it, and the better it understands your business.

104|104|104|104|

The mistake to avoid at all costs

105|105|105|105|

Content must stay human

Many businesses think it's enough to ask ChatGPT to write articles. That's a mistake. AI doesn't reward auto-generated content: it favors useful content, real experiences, practical advice and businesses that demonstrate their expertise. AI is an assistant, not an author.

106|106|106|106|

Why a blog becomes a competitive edge

107|107|107|107|

Each new article answers a new question. Each answer attracts new visitors. Each visit strengthens your visibility. Gradually, your business becomes a reference on its topic. That's exactly what AI-powered engines look for: sources able to explain a field clearly, and it strengthens your AI visibility too.

108|108|108|108|

The businesses that will be visible tomorrow

109|109|109|109|

The businesses that succeed tomorrow will share several traits. They'll publish regularly, they'll answer their customers' questions, they'll demonstrate their expertise, they'll maintain their reputation, they'll continuously improve their site and they'll build their visibility as a lasting asset.
