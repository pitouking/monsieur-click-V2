# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary: local TPE owners and independents who need more clients from Google and AI answers, without buying SEO jargon or isolated deliverables. They arrive mid-problem (invisible, weak site, wasted ads) and want a clear next step.

Secondary: Paris / Île-de-France searchers for “agence SEO”, “consultant SEO”, or similar who compare providers. Speak to them with authority and proof, without diluting the primary local-business voice.

## Product Purpose

Monsieur Click helps local businesses get found, understood, and chosen through the Click First™ system (site, local presence, content, measurement). Success is qualified diagnostics and Score Click First™ completions that lead to serious conversations, not vanity traffic or soft newsletter signups.

## Positioning

Click First™ is one system for being found, understood, and chosen—not a menu of isolated SEO or web prestations.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Calculer mon Score Click First™
- Secondary CTA: Obtenir mon diagnostic gratuit
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: Click First™ : un système pour être trouvé, compris, choisi.
- Belief ladder: (1) a concrete score and diagnostic beat vague SEO quotes; (2) one integrated system beats isolated prestations. Both must land before the primary CTA.
- Proof on hand: Google reviews / client ratings; client case studies and before/after; documented Click First™ method (score, process, service pages). Drop files into `.impeccable/assets/proof/` when sharing assets for craft work.

## Brand Personality

Clair · Concret · Expert. Tone: confident and clear without aggressive hype. French copy uses vouvoiement, business-local concreteness, and no empty promises (no guaranteed #1 ranking). The visitor is the hero; Monsieur Click is the guide.

## Anti-references

Not a SEO factory (cookie-cutter city×service doorways, generic agency fluff). Not SaaS marketing slop (purple gradients, pill spam, fake social proof, dashboard cosplay on a marketing site).

## Design Principles

1. One page, one job—lead to score or diagnostic, not a service catalog dump.
2. Show the system before selling the bricks.
3. Proof before promise—reviews, cases, and method before claims.
4. Expert without jargon or hype.
5. Practice what you preach—fast, readable, local-credible pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Reasonable best practices (contrast, focus, semantic HTML, respect prefers-reduced-motion). No formal WCAG certification target.
