# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary: local SMB owners and independents who need more clients from Google and AI answers, without buying SEO jargon or isolated deliverables. They arrive mid-problem (invisible, weak site, wasted ads) and want a clear next step.

Secondary: searchers comparing local SEO or web design providers. Speak with authority and proof, without diluting the primary local-business voice.

## Product Purpose

Monsieur Click helps local businesses get found, understood, and chosen through the Click First™ system (site, local presence, content, measurement). Success is qualified diagnostics and Score Click First™ completions that lead to serious conversations, not vanity traffic or soft newsletter signups.

## Positioning

Click First™ is one system for being found, understood, and chosen, not a menu of isolated SEO or web deliverables.

## Conversion & proof

### English (.com)

- Primary CTA: Calculate my Click First™ Score → `/click-first#score`
- Secondary CTA: Get my free diagnostic → `#parlons` (homepage form)
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: Click First™: one system to get found, understood, chosen.
- Belief ladder: (1) a concrete score and diagnostic beat vague SEO quotes; (2) one integrated system beats isolated deliverables. Both must land before the primary CTA.
- Footer / contact page may keep Free diagnostic → `/contact`. Do not route homepage primary CTAs to `#contact` (banner) when the form is `#parlons`.

### French (.fr)

- Primary CTA: Calculer mon Score Click First™
- Secondary CTA: Obtenir mon diagnostic gratuit
- 10-second line: Click First™ : un système pour être trouvé, compris, choisi.

## Brand Personality

Clear · Concrete · Expert. Tone: confident and clear without aggressive hype. English copy is direct and business-local. No empty promises (no guaranteed #1 ranking). The visitor is the hero; Monsieur Click is the guide.

## Anti-references

Not a SEO factory (cookie-cutter city×service doorways, generic agency fluff). Not SaaS marketing slop (purple gradients, pill spam, fake social proof, dashboard cosplay on a marketing site).

## Design Principles

1. One page, one job: lead to score or diagnostic, not a service catalog dump.
2. Show the system before selling the bricks.
3. Proof before promise: reviews, cases, and method before claims.
4. Expert without jargon or hype.
5. Practice what you preach: fast, readable, local-credible pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Reasonable best practices (contrast, focus, semantic HTML, respect prefers-reduced-motion). No formal WCAG certification target.
